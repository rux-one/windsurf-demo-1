About Oniet

Welcome to Oniet, your premier source for news with a nostalgic twist! Established in the groundbreaking year of 1999, Oniet was founded with a simple mission: to deliver the news as it was, when it was. We believe that understanding the past is key to navigating the present, and what better way to do that than through the lens of contemporary news reporting from bygone eras?

Our dedicated team of retro-journalists scours digital archives and dusty microfilms to bring you authentic news articles, features, and op-eds from the late 20th century. From the Y2K bug persiapan to the rise of boy bands, from dial-up internet to the latest Tamagotchi craze, we cover it all with the accuracy and charm of the period.

Our Philosophy

At Oniet, we're not just about reporting old news. We're about preserving a moment in time. We believe that the way news was presented tells us as much about the era as the news itself. Why "Oniet"?

The name "Oniet" is a playful nod to the early days of the internet, where unique and quirky names were all the rage. It sounds a bit like "on it," because we are indeed "on it" when it comes to digging up the past. Plus, it was available as a domain name back in '99, and some things are just too good to change!
