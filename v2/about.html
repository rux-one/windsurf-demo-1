About Oniet

Welcome to Oniet, your premier (and perhaps only) source for news delivered with that distinct early 2000s digital charm! We were founded on the revolutionary idea that information could be shared globally through this exciting new medium called the "World Wide Web."

Our mission is simple: to provide timely updates on world events, local happenings, and sports scores, all formatted for your 800x600 screen resolution. We believe in the power of pixelated fonts and the aesthetic appeal of table-based layouts.

Our Philosophy

At Oniet, we're not afraid to embrace the limitations of technology. In fact, we celebrate them! Why worry about "responsive design" when you can simply tell users the optimal viewing conditions? Our crack team of webmasters (a very prestigious title back then) works tirelessly with dial-up modems to bring you content that loads... eventually.
